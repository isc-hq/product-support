# 📘 Support & Community Help

Welcome to the **official support hub** for our products.  
This repository is the public place where you can:

- Report problems  
- Ask questions  
- Get help from the community  
- Share ideas and feedback  

If you’re looking for help, you’re in the right place.

---

## 🚀 How to Get Help

### 🐞 **Report a Problem**  
If something isn’t working as expected, open an Issue.  
Please include:

- Steps to reproduce  
- Expected vs. actual behavior  
- Screenshots or logs (remove sensitive info)  
- Product version / environment details  

Clear reports help us resolve issues faster.

---

### 💬 **Ask a Question**  
Use Discussions for:

- Troubleshooting help  
- “How do I…?” questions  
- Best practices  
- Advice from other users  

This is the best place for community‑driven support.

---

### 💡 **Suggest a Feature**  
Have an idea that would improve one of our products?  
We’d love to hear it.

---

## 📚 Documentation & Resources

- **FAQ** — answers to common questions  
- **Troubleshooting Guide** — step‑by‑step fixes  
- **Known Issues** — things we’re already aware of

## 🧭 What This Repo Is *Not*

To protect our intellectual property and internal systems:

- This repo **does not contain product source code**  
- This repo **does not expose private repositories**  
- This repo **is not for security vulnerabilities**  
- This repo **is not for account or billing issues**

For security reports, please follow our **Security Policy**.

---

## ⏱️ Response Expectations

We aim to respond to new Issues and Discussions within:

- **48–72 hours** for general support  
- **Sooner** for high‑impact problems  

Community members may respond even faster.

- Stay on topic  
- Do not share private or sensitive information  
- Follow our **Code of Conduct**

We reserve the right to moderate content that violates these guidelines.
